Bulk Density of Cuttings (Using Mud Balance)


Procedure

  1. Cuttings must be washed free of mud. In an oil mud, diesel oil can be

   used instead of water.

  1. Set mud balance at 8.33 ppg.
  2. Fill the mud balance with cuttings until a balance is obtained with the

   lid in place.

  1. Remove lid, fill cup with water (cuttings included), replace lid, and

   dry outside of mud balance.

  1. Move counterweight to obtain new balance.
